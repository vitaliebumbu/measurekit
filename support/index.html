Frequently asked questions

How do I add a measurement to a photo?

Open a room photo, make sure the photo is unlocked (tap the lock icon if needed), then tap and drag on the photo to draw a dimension arrow. You can add angle notes, text notes, and voice notes from the toolbar.

Are my measurements accurate?

Measure Kit Pro is a visual documentation tool for photos and manually entered measurements. It does not physically measure objects for you. Always confirm critical measurements with a physical measuring tool before ordering materials, cutting, installing, or building.

Where is my data stored?

Your projects, photos, annotations, and voice notes are stored on your device. The app does not include third-party analytics or advertising. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I export a project?

Open a project and use the export option to generate a PDF of your rooms, photos, and annotations. You can then share it through the iOS share sheet.

Why does the app ask for camera, microphone, photo, or location access?

Camera and photo library access let you capture or import room photos. Microphone access lets you record voice notes. Location access is optional and is only used to tag a project with an address when you enable it. You can decline any permission and still use the core features.

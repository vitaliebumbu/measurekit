Measure Kit Pro Support Privacy Terms

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

Measure Kit Pro is designed to help you document rooms with photos, measurements, notes, voice notes, and PDF exports. We respect your privacy and keep data collection to a minimum.

Information the app uses

Measure Kit Pro may use the following information when you choose to grant access:

  • Camera — to capture room photos.
  • Photo library — to import room photos.
  • Microphone — to record voice notes attached to photos.
  • Location — to tag projects with an address when you enable geolocation. This is optional.

Storage

Project data, photos, annotations, and voice notes are stored on your device. Measure Kit Pro does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs, and we do not operate servers that collect your project content.

Sharing

Measure Kit Pro can create PDF exports from your project data. You control when and where those files are shared through the iOS share sheet or other system sharing options.

Tracking

Measure Kit Pro does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Children's privacy

Measure Kit Pro is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at vitalie.bumbu@gmail.com.
